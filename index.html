During their trip through the Tower, characters deal with monstrous threats and ancient traps, as well as constructs, summons, necromancy and crazy wizard magic.

Adventure Hooks

Adventurers can find the Tower within a close vicinity to City of Light northern wall and to the village. The characters can be drawn to the dungeon for any of the following reasons.

Going for Glory

You are eager to make a name for yourself. The legend of the Tower is well known locally, and stories indicate it is a place that holds promise for those intent on discovery, glory, and treasure!

Rescue Mission

Another party of adventurers, locally based, delved into the Sunless Citadel a month past. They were never seen again. Two human members of that ill fated-party were brother and sister.

Solving a Mystery

There is something Necrotically wrong with the Tower, but what is it?
